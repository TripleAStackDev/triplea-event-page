Upcoming Events

TripleCon 2025

TripleCon 2025 is our flagship tech and creativity conference designed for emerging developers, digital creatives, and startup founders. This year’s theme is “Code. Create. Connect.” Expect keynote speeches from international devs, breakout workshops on full-stack tools, and sessions focused on freelancing, remote work, and real-world software projects. Attendees will have opportunities to demo their products, pitch ideas, and network with potential clients and collaborators. The energy will be electric, and the knowledge shared — unforgettable.

TripleHack 2.0

TripleHack 2.0 is an intense 48-hour hackathon experience tailored for Africa’s most ambitious young coders and designers. You’ll form a team, choose a problem, and build a solution — all in just two days. From AI-powered apps to community-based platforms, the event will showcase raw talent, teamwork, and tech innovation. Cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, and even internship slots are up for grabs. If you love pressure and creativity, this is your arena.

Past Events

TripleHack 2024

The debut edition of TripleHack held in July 2024 brought together 120+ participants from across Nigeria and Ghana. With themes like Education, HealthTech, and Rural Innovation, teams pushed the limits of what can be built in a weekend. The winning team developed a mobile app that helps out-of-school youth access free learning paths using WhatsApp bots. Judges praised the creativity, resilience, and teamwork shown by all groups. The success of this first edition inspired the bigger 2025 edition.

TripleMeet 2024

TripleMeet 2024 was a laid-back community event designed to bring together devs, designers, and digital builders to simply vibe, connect, and share their journeys. Held outdoors with food, chill music, and lightning talks, it created room for people to link up in person after months of building alone. It was a safe space to rant about bugs, share project struggles, and even meet future collaborators. Some say it was the soul of the TripleA community.
